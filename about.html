About Fishels Frozen Treats

About Us

Welcome to Fishel’s Frozen Treats, your neighborhood destination for the finest handcrafted frozen desserts! Located in the heart of our charming lakeside town, Fishel’s has been serving smiles and scoops for over three generations.

Our Story

Founded by the Fishel family decades ago, our shop began as a small stand by the lake, offering locals a refreshing escape from the summer heat. Today, we've grown into a beloved community gathering spot, where visitors of all ages come to enjoy our artisanal ice creams, sorbets, and specialty frozen delights. Each recipe is crafted with love, using locally sourced ingredients and family traditions passed down through the years.
